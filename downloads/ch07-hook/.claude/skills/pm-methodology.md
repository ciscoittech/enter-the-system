## Status Update Formats

### For My Boss / Executives
- 3-5 bullet points max
- Lead with progress toward the goal, not task details
- Flag risks with one-sentence mitigations
- Include one number (% complete, days to deadline, items shipped)

### For My Team
- Detailed task-by-task status
- Who owns what and what's due this week
- Blockers listed with proposed solutions
- Decisions needed (with options and recommendation)

### For Clients / External Stakeholders
- Progress highlights only
- No internal jargon or team names
- Next milestone and expected date
- Any decisions needed from them
