# Prompt Rating System

## Difficulty Rating (1-5)

How hard is this prompt to set up and use?

| Rating | Label | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Copy-paste | Type it exactly as written, works on any files |
| 2 | Fill-in-blanks | Replace 1-2 placeholders with your specifics |
| 3 | Some setup | Need to organize files or describe your situation first |
| 4 | Moderate setup | Need to prepare files, understand the workflow, maybe tweak |
| 5 | Advanced | Multiple steps, requires understanding from earlier chapters |

## Effectiveness Rating (1-5)

How good is the output?

| Rating | Label | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Unreliable | Output needs significant rework more than half the time |
| 2 | Rough draft | Usable as a starting point but needs editing |
| 3 | Good enough | Output is usable with minor tweaks |
| 4 | High quality | Output is ready to use with minimal review |
| 5 | Excellent | Output consistently matches or exceeds what you'd do manually |

## Rating Combinations That Matter

| Difficulty | Effectiveness | Verdict |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| 1-2 | 4-5 | **Best prompts** — include early in the book |
| 1-2 | 2-3 | Include with caveats about reviewing output |
| 3-4 | 4-5 | Include in later chapters (more advanced readers) |
| 3-4 | 1-2 | Exclude or adapt heavily |
| 5 | any | Only include if the use case is important enough |
| any | 1 | Exclude |

## Chapter Placement Guidelines

- Chapters 1-3 (foundations): Only difficulty 1-2 prompts
- Chapters 4-8 (single tasks): Difficulty 1-3, effectiveness 3+
- Chapters 9-11 (domain): Difficulty 2-4, domain-specific
- Chapters 12-14 (advanced): Any difficulty, effectiveness 3+
