# Voice Reference — Frontier Engineering Cowork

## AI Tells to Eliminate

### Structural Tells
- Numbered lists as the primary teaching method (use prose with steps embedded)
- "Firstly / Secondly / Thirdly"
- "Let's dive in" / "Let's get started" / "Let's explore"
- "Key takeaways" / "In summary" / "To summarize"
- Conclusions that restate the introduction
- Perfectly balanced paragraphs (vary length deliberately)
- Every section the same length

### Phrase Tells — NEVER use these
- "Additionally" / "Moreover" / "Furthermore"
- "It's important to note" / "It's worth mentioning"
- "One might argue" / "Some would say"
- "At the end of the day"
- "Leverage" / "Utilize" / "Delve" / "Navigate"
- "Ecosystem" / "Robust" / "Scalable" / "Seamless"
- "Game-changing" / "Revolutionary" / "Cutting-edge"
- "Landscape" / "Journey" / "Unlock"
- "In conclusion" / "To wrap up"
- "As we've seen" / "As mentioned earlier"

### Tone Tells
- Relentless positivity ("This amazing tool will transform your work!")
- Both-sides-ing ("While some prefer X, others find Y equally valid")
- Hedging every claim ("It could potentially maybe sometimes help")
- Thanking the reader ("Thank you for reading this chapter")
- Excessive enthusiasm ("I'm so excited to show you this!")
- Corporate neutrality ("This solution provides value across use cases")

## Voice Markers to Inject

### Rhythm
- Short punch followed by longer explanation
- Fragments for emphasis. Like this.
- Max 1 rhetorical question per chapter section
- Vary paragraph length: 1 sentence, then 3 sentences, then 2

### Vocabulary — USE these
- "Here's the thing" (once per chapter max)
- "That's it. That's the whole system."
- "The part that actually matters is..."
- "You'll know it worked because..."
- "Most guides will tell you [X]. Skip that."
- "What Claude gives you back is..."
- Exact numbers ("saves you about 40 minutes" not "saves significant time")

### Personality
- Honest about what doesn't work ("Claude is bad at [X]. Don't bother.")
- Specific about payoff ("I used this to process 3 months of bank statements in 6 minutes")
- Impatient with complexity that doesn't serve the reader
- Respects the reader's time — every sentence earns its place
- Dry humor, sparingly (one good joke beats five forced ones)

## Transformation Examples

### Before (AI voice):
"It's important to leverage Claude Cowork's robust file organization capabilities to streamline your digital workspace. By utilizing the powerful AI-driven sorting functionality, you can efficiently navigate your documents and achieve a seamless organizational ecosystem."

### After (Book voice):
"You have 2,000 files in your Downloads folder. Some are from 2019. Claude can sort them in about 4 minutes. Here's how."

### Before (AI voice):
"To optimize your prompt engineering approach, consider incorporating contextual parameters that align with your desired output specifications, ensuring the AI model has sufficient information to generate comprehensive and accurate deliverables."

### After (Book voice):
"Tell Claude four things: who you are, what the files are, what you want back, and what to do when something's unclear. That's the whole formula. Every prompt in this book follows it."
